    Below is a list of equipment that is allowed to be used in competition.

    Equipment Name Description
    Weightlifting Belt A belt that goes around the waist of the athlete to create a more stable trunk through generating more intra-abdominal pressure.
    Weightlifting Shoes A shoe designed for olympic style weightlifting. The heels are raised to artifically give the athlete greater ankle mobility. Furthermore, the shoes offer great stability for the athlete during the lift due to wide, flat base.
    Knee Wraps/Sleeves A fabric that goes around the knees of the athlete. They offer both warmth to the joint, and sometimes add to the stretch reflex of the joint for added stability.
    Wrist Wraps A fabric that wraps around the wrists of the athlete in order to add support to the joint as it supports the weight in the overhead position.
    Chalk Substance that is used for the hands to remove sweat, and add additional grip to the bar.
